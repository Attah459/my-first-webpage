What Makes this such an interesting moment?

    If Ken had blocked any of the attacks he would have died by chip damage, and hence parrying was his best option
    By choosing to parry, he had to give up blocking, and doing the multiple inputs are hard with tournament nerves
    If Ken did any other combo, he wouldn't have won. In such a tight scenerio, he picked the optimal combo route.
